Civic User Testing Group

The Civic User Testing Group (CUTGroup) is a community of residents who get paid to test civic websites and apps.

Thank you!

Thanks for signing up to be a member of the CUTGroup. You should receive a text message to verify your account. Once your account has been verified we will send you a $5 VISA gift card within a week. If you have any questions, please write Sonja Marziano of the Smart Chicago Collaborative at smarziano@cct.org.
